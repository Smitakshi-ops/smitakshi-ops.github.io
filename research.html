Research

My research focuses on novel semiconducting materials for photovoltaic and infrared applications, with an emphasis on sustainable absorber materials and their optoelectronic behavior.

  • Zintl-phase phosphides for next-generation photovoltaics
  • Thin-film fabrication and doping strategies
  • Photoluminescence and Raman spectroscopy of semiconductors
  • Defect physics, carrier dynamics, and recombination mechanisms
